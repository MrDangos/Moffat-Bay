About Us

Moffat Bay Lodge is a premier destination on the scenic Joviedsa Island in the San Juan Islands. Developed with the approval of the First Nations Development Committee, our lodge reflects a commitment to sustainability and natural beauty of the bay.

We offer guests a peaceful retreat surrounded by beautiful forests and breathtaking coastal views, Whether you're here for relaxation, adventure, or cultural exploration, Moffat Bay Lodge can provide a unique unforgettable experience.

Our Vision

The development of Moffat Bay Lodge was guided by a vision to create a harmonious balance between modern comfort and environmental stewardship. The First Nations Development Committee ensured that all aspects of the resort honor the land, traditions, and history of the region.

What We Offer

  • Luxury accommodation with stunning views
  • Outdoor activities
  • Fine dining
  • Access to marina services
  • Guided tours and fishing trips
