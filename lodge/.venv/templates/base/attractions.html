Island Attractions

Moffat Bay has something for everyone. From peaceful hikes to underwater adventures, the island is full of ways to make your stay unforgettable.

Things To Do

Hiking

Explore scenic trails through forests and coastal cliffs

Kayaking

Paddle through calm waters and hidden sea caves along the shoreline. Rentals and guided tours available for all skill levels.

Wildlife Watching

Join a guided boat tour to see humpback and orca whales up close. Tours depart daily from the marina and run about three hours.

Scuba and Snorkeling

Discover kelp forests and marine life beneath the surface. Certified guides lead dives for both beginners and experienced divers.
